Standard Gym Exercises

A standard gym course typically includes elements such as warm-up exercises, cardio workouts, strength training using machines or free weights, cool-down stretches, and sometimes group fitness classes.

  • Weightlifting
  • Cardio (e.g., running, cycling)
  • Bodyweight exercises (e.g., push-ups, squats)
  • Stretching
  • Aerobics
